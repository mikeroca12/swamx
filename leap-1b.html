Perforated facesheet inspection. Zones A through F defined by distance from leading edge. Zone A starts at 1.00 inch (25.4 mm), Zone C extends to 4.00 inches (101.6 mm). Upper visible surface is primary inspection area.

Key dimensions: Zone A = 1.00 inch (25.4 mm) from edge. Zone E upper = 3.50 inches (88.9 mm). Zone F = 1.00 inch (25.4 mm). Zone C = 4.00 inches (101.6 mm). Construction: fiberglass honeycomb core between outer barrel skin panel (aluminum) and inner barrel acoustic panel perforated facesheet. Inlet cowl lipskin is aluminum.

Fan Case Module Acoustic Shroud
AMM 72-21-00-200-802-G00 / G.(2)
▼
NOTE: If the inlet cowl and the acoustic shroud are installed, do not remove them.
Seal Examination Criteria
  • Missing bonded seal: Permitted if not more than 39.37 in. (1000 mm) in cumulative length.
  • Cut, worn through, and torn seal: Cut the seal when it is visible in the flowpath. Permitted if not more than 39.37 in. (1000 mm) in cumulative length.
  • Crazing in seal: Permitted.
PREVIOUS EA: A/C 8779 TTG8Q00W9435 - APPLY SPD TAPE
Construction: Inner surface has glass fiber coating areas. Structure consists of carbon plies with protective coating. Outer and inner surfaces identified in legend.
